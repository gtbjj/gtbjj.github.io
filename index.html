(noun) | A state of being

The transient state of oscillating between desires for serenity and chaos amid fulfillment of both creative and destructive energies ultimately coalescing in the relentless, simultaneous pursuit and challenging of human nature. The line between enlightenment and anarchy is thin. You can be both a warrior and be at peace. Embrace that violence is always an option and savor life's tranquil moments. *Amor fati*
